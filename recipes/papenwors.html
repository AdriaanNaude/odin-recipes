Pap en Wors

Ingredients:

  • 1 Kg boerewors
  • 250 ml Corn flour
  • 650 ml water
  • Cup of dised cheddar chees
  • Can of tomato and onion smoors
  • 4 et brandsous

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to (180 degrees C)
  2. Squirt or spread around oven glaspan
  3. Fold wors in a sirkel and cook in heated pan till brown
  4. Remove from pan and set aside
  5. Mix the corn flour with 259 ml water to a past
  6. Heat the rest of water in a casarol en add the corn flour when the water is at cooking point
  7. Add salt en stir till cooking point and until past is thick
  8. Steam till cooked
  9. Stir a little chees in mix
  10. Skoop the paste out into the the plate
  11. Skoop the wors, still in a sirkel on top
  12. Heat the tomato and onion smoors with the chilliesals
  13. Add salt and pepper and cook for 5 minuts
  14. Skoop sals over wors and spread last of cheese onto wors and pap
  15. Cook for 20 minuts until warm
