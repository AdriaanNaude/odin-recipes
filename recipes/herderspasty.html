Herderspasty

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Sour cream
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Corn medley
  • flour
  • Beef broth
  • Tomato paste
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaf

Steps:

    Filling:

  1. Add 1 ground Beef spread across plate
  2. Add 1 small Onion and dice it into Beef
  3. Add 2 cloves Garlic, minced into beef
  4. Add 2 cups of frozen Peas, Carrots and Corn medley
  5. Add 2 tsp of Flour
  6. Add 1/2 cup of Beef Broth
  7. Add in Tomato paste and Worcestershire sauce
  8. Add 1 tsp of Rosemary
  9. Add 1/2 tsp of Thyme
  10. Then add 1 Bay leaf

    11. Mashed Potatoes Topping:

  11. Add 2 pounds Potatoes peeled and cut into big chuncks
  12. Add 4 tbsp of Butter (half a stick)
  13. Add 1/4 cup of Sour cream
  14. Add 1/4 to 1/2 cup of milk
  15. Then Salt and Pepper
